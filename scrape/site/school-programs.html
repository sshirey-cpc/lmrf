Programs

Our programs are designed to build a connection between participants and the natural beauty of the Mississippi River. We offer programming in schools, on the shore and on the water.

In Class

Our educators deliver hands-on lessons aligned to Arkansas and Mississippi state standards, bringing the river right into the classroom. Students explore river ecology, history, and conservation through engaging, interactive activities.

View In-Class Programming Details

Meet the Mississippi (Day Trips)

Take a short canoe trip to a secluded island or beach and use the river as a classroom for the day. Students can also explore the Mississippi from the riverbank, learning about its ecology and history up close.

These day trips provide a powerful first encounter with the river and inspire a deeper connection to the natural world.

Dive Deeper (Overnight Trips)

Spend one night on a Mississippi River island for an immersive outdoor experience. Students participate in lessons on river history, ecology, wilderness skills, and camping.

Overnight trips allow participants to truly connect with the river — watching the sunset over the water, listening to the sounds of the island at night, and waking up surrounded by the wild beauty of the Mississippi.
