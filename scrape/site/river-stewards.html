About the Program

Protecting Our River

The River Stewards are youth who want to actively participate in promoting the health of the river. They meet after school during the year to become experts on the river and plan events that connect their community to the river.

Through hands-on learning and community engagement, River Stewards develop a deep understanding of the Mississippi River ecosystem and become advocates for its protection.

Become a River Steward!

Are you adventurous? Do you want to learn more about nature and how to protect it? Join the River Stewards and make a difference for the Mississippi River.

The River Stewards Program is currently only available in Helena. We hope to expand the program in the future!
