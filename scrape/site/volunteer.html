Volunteer

Volunteer Opportunities

Afterschool Programs

Tues/Thurs 3–6pm

Help kids with homework, games, and outdoor skills. Our afterschool programs run twice a week and provide a safe, engaging environment for Delta youth to learn and grow.

Field Trips

During school hours

Assist during school hours with Mississippi River field trips. Help students experience the river firsthand through guided excursions and hands-on learning activities.

Fundraising and Marketing

Help with event planning and promotion. Support LMRF by assisting with fundraising campaigns, community events, social media, and outreach efforts.
