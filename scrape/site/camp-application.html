Mississippi Summer Leadership Camp Application

Program Details

The Mississippi Summer Leadership Camp is a 4-day canoe expedition covering approximately 20 miles per day on the Lower Mississippi River. This transformative experience is designed for high school students in grades 9–12.


  • Trip 1: June 14–17
  • Trip 2: June 21–24
  • Cost: $1,000 per student (90% scholarships available for Arkansas and Mississippi Delta residents, bringing your cost to just $100 out of pocket)
  • Deposit: $20 to secure your spot
  • No prior paddling experience or swimming ability required

You can also download the PDF application and submit it by mail or email.
