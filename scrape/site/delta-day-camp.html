Delta Adventure Day Camp

Camp Details

  • Dates: June 10–14, 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM
  • Ages: Grades 2–6
  • Location: 107 Perry St, Helena, AR (Helena River Park entrance)
  • Fee: $25
  • Contact: Shannon McMulkin, (870) 753-8954

Activities

Campers will enjoy a week of outdoor adventure and learning, including:


  • Hiking
  • Kayaking
  • Biking
  • Swimming
  • Camping skills
  • Boat safety

Logistics

  • Meals provided: Breakfast, lunch, and snack
  • Drop-off: 7:30–8:00 AM
  • Pick-up: 3:00–3:30 PM
