Summer Programs

Join us for these programs in Summer of 2025. Contact us for more information.

Summer Leadership Camp

A 4-day, 100-mile Mississippi River expedition designed for high school students. This immersive experience focuses on leadership, teamwork, and building a deep connection to the river.

Trip 1: June 2–5, 2025 (Girls)

Trip 2: June 9–12, 2025 (Boys)

Participants will paddle through some of the most beautiful and remote stretches of the Lower Mississippi, camping on river islands and developing skills they will carry with them for a lifetime.

Delta Adventure Day Camp

Postponed for 2020 — Date TBD

A week-long exploration of the Delta by bike, foot, and canoe. This day camp is open to 2nd through 6th graders and offers young adventurers a chance to discover the natural wonders right in their own backyard.

Campers spend each day outdoors, learning about the river ecosystem, developing outdoor skills, and making lasting memories with new friends.
