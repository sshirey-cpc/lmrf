River Stewards

The River Stewards are youth who want to actively participate in promoting the health of the river. They meet after school during the year to become experts on the river and plan events that connect their community to the river

Become a River Steward!

Are you adventurous? Do you want to learn more about nature and how to protect it? If this sounds like you sign up to be a River Steward this year!

*The River Stewards Program is only currently only available in Helena, we hope to expand the program in the future!
