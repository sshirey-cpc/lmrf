Benefits of Membership

  • LMRF's quarterly newsletter with news and updates from the Lower Mississippi

  • Invitations to special events

  • Recognition of your gift in our newsletter

  • 10% off LMRF merchandise

  • 10& off the price of Community Paddle trips

 
 

Volunteer

Current Volunteer Opportunities

  • -Afterschool Programs- Tuesday and Thursday from 3-6. Volunteers can help with homework, lead games, and activities or teach outdoor skills.

  • Field Trips- Volunteers help deliver programs about the river to small groups.  Usually during the school day so a great opportunity if you have a flexible schedule.

  • Fundraising and Marketing-  There are various ways you can help us raise funds for LMRF from folding letters to planning events.
